My Experience in Japan

My trip to Japan began with a breathtaking view from Tokyo Skytree, where I gazed out over the endless city below, a vast sea of buildings and lights. From there, I plunged into Tokyo's vibrant energy, crossing the famous Shibuya Crossing amid a wave of people—a stark contrast to the peace I later found in Kyoto's serene temples, like the tranquil grounds of Fushimi Inari. Riding the bullet train between these cities felt like stepping between two worlds—one buzzing with modern life and the other steeped in tradition. Whether navigating the crowds or feeding deer in Nara Park, every moment in Japan was a blend of contrasts that made the journey unforgettable.
