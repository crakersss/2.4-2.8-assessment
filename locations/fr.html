My Experience in France

France was an unforgettable blend of history, culture, and excitement. In Lyon, I had the amazing opportunity to watch the Rugby World Cup match between Italy and New Zealand, which added an electric atmosphere to the trip. From there, I ventured to Paris, where iconic landmarks like the Eiffel Tower and the Louvre truly lived up to their reputation. Standing in the bustling Place de la Concorde, surrounded by centuries of history, was a surreal experience. The food throughout my trip—from croissants to traditional French dishes—was simply unforgettable, making France a feast for both the senses and the soul.
